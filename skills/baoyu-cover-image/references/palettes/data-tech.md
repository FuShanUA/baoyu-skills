# Data/Tech (Corporate B2B) Palette

**Keywords**: tech, data, ai, corporate, b2b, warm-tech, crisp

A professional palette tailored for modern B2B technology, data governance, and artificial intelligence themes. Focuses on bright, clean, and highly professional aesthetics. Absolutely NO dark, heavy, or gloomy colors.

**Primary Colors**:
- Deep Navy (`#0A192F`) or Crisp Indigo (`#1A365D`)
- Warm Amber (`#FFBF00`) or Vivid Orange (`#FF851B`)
- Clean White (`#FFFFFF`) or Tech Silver (`#F0F4F8`)

**Accent Colors**:
- Bright Teal (`#39CCCC`) or Soft Cyan (`#E0F7FA`)
- Muted Gold (`#D4AF37`)

**Application**:
- Create a breathable, pristine tech aesthetic
- Use the warm colors (Amber/Orange) to provide humanity and energy
- Use the cool colors (Navy/Teal) for structure and data representation
- Ensure high contrast for readability and a premium corporate feel
