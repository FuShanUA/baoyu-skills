# Corporate Tech (B2B Data) Style

**Keywords**: tech, b2b, data, pristine, clean, modern, flat-vector

A pristine, highly professional vector style tailored for modern B2B SaaS, data architecture, and enterprise technology. The style emphasizes clarity, structured data layers, and an extremely clean visual hierarchy. Absolutely NO dark, heavy, or purely decorative elements that don't serve a structural purpose. 

**Visual Characteristics**:
- **Line & Form**: Crisp vector shapes, geometric precision, and balanced proportions. Flowing tech connectors and elegant nodes.
- **Lighting & Depth**: Flat with subtle, polished drop-shadows or delicate glowing nodes (micro-shadows).
- **Texture**: Clean, solid color fills with entirely breathable whitespace. No grunge or noisy gradients.

**Color Palette**:
- Must align with the Corporate B2B Data identity.
- Cool & Clean: Deep Navy (`#0A192F`), Crisp Teal (`#39CCCC`), Electric Blue.
- Warm Humanity: Warm Amber (`#FFBF00`) or Vivid Orange (`#FF851B`) for accents.
- Backgrounds: Tech Silver (`#F0F4F8`) or Pristine White (`#FFFFFF`). No dark canvases.

**Application Notes**:
- **Layout Compatibility**: Ideal for `bento-grid`, `hierarchical-layers`, and `binary-comparison`.
- **Icons**: Use universally recognized, minimalist tech icons (servers, gears, shields, clouds, network hubs).
- **Text**: STRICTLY Simplified Chinese for all labels. NO English terms. Highly legible typography.
